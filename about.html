Our Startup Journey

From a simple API idea to serving hundreds of enterprises globally.

JANUARY 2023

The Spark

Nexus was conceived by Sarah Jenkins and David Chen in a small apartment. The prototype handled 1,000 tasks/hour with simple webhook loops.

AUGUST 2023

Pre-Seed & Beta Launch

Secured $1.5M in pre-seed funding. Launched closed beta to 100 technology startups, optimizing routing speeds to less than 20ms.

MAY 2024

Series A & Global Expansion

Raised a $12M Series A round. Introduced natural language automation prompts, SOC2 certification compliance, and expanded the core engineer team.

PRESENT DAY

Scaling the Horizon

Nexus now orchestrates over 15 million operational tasks per day for 350+ enterprises, rolling out AI-driven autonomous agent builders.
